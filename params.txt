Possible Parameters:

INFO/VERIFY:

-i	(info)

-v	(verify)

-pc	(PRG Count)

-cc	(CHR Count)

EXTRACTION:

-xp	(extract PRG)

-xc 	(extract CHR)

-xs	(extract sprites from CHR)

-xsp	(extract sprites from PRG)


INJECTION:

-ip	(inject PRG)

-ic	(inject CHR)

-is	(inject sprites into CHR)

-isp	(inject sprites into PRG)

TITLES:

-st	(set title)

-rt	(remove title)

-pt	(print title)

MISC:

-ds	(draw sprites)

-f	(set source file)
